My story

The path that brought me here.

I was among the top students of my generation and recognized for academic excellence. After four years of clinical practice, I now work as a gynecologist at the Health Center Nis.

Alongside clinical work, I'm developing my academic path through doctoral studies, lectures and professional writing. I am now approaching the choice of my doctoral dissertation direction.
